  • About Me!

    Hello, I’m Max Beauchesne, a Vancouver-based illustrator. My greatest pleasure is creating work that brings joy to people’s lives. Bold, punchy, and colourful illustrations are my go-to style. When I’m not busy illustrating, you’ll find me beach combing, exploring nature, or photographing local wildlife. These interests all help inspire my work.

    My strengths are character design, concept art, storyboarding, and motion for illustration. I’ll be graduating from Capilano University’s IDEA School of Design in 2024 and looking for work. If you enjoy my work and need an illustrator for a project let's connect.
