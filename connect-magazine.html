Connect Magazine

Illustrated Magazine

Connect is a magazine centred on providing young children and families an opportunity to understand the world around them in a fun, safe, and creative way.

Through colourful visually appealing graphics kids are encouraged to learn about diversity, kindness, respect, and understanding towards others and themselves.

This content encourages a space for creativity, inspiration, and growth.
